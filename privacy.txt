Privacy Policy

WiFile never transfers your files outside your local network. Data is transferred directly between mobile device and computer. 
Access to files on your device is needed to share them to your computer. 
Transferring data is encrypted using AES-256 (by SSH2 protocol).

For the purpose of improving application quality WiFile sends annonymous data about errors and crashes to the Google's service Firebase.

If you have any questions about WiFile privacy, please contact us at support@wifile.info
